Portfolio Summary

Core interpretation

The calculator now treats a multi-property sale as a portfolio event. Long-term capital gain brackets and NIIT are applied after aggregating taxable gain, rather than treating every property as an isolated transaction.

Depreciation recapture is still tracked property-by-property because each property may carry a different basis, land allocation, depreciation history, and passive-loss offset.
